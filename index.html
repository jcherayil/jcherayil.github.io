About

Hey there! I’m Jess, a mobile app developer currently based in Nashville, Tennessee. I recently graduated from Wellesley College, where I received my degree in Computer Science and French.

As a pretty new developer, I’m keen to explore multiple fields in the technology sector. Though I got my start in mobile app development, I’m excited to branch out and see what else is out there. I’m particularly interested in natural language processing, a perfect intersection of my passions - languages and technology.

When I’m not at work, you can find me jamming on my guitar, learning new languages (currently, American Sign Language and Malayalam), or playing ultimate frisbee.

Experience

    CS111 Teaching Assistant

    I spent two semesters helping college students learn to code. This meant brushing up on my Python skills, holding weekly office hours, attending lectures and labs, grading homework assignments, and individually tutoring.
